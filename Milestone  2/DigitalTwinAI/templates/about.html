What is Digital Twin AI?

Digital Twin AI is an intelligent personal decision-support system that creates a digital representation of your habits, behaviours, finances, study patterns, and lifestyle choices. By leveraging predictive analytics, machine learning, and generative AI, the platform forecasts future outcomes and provides personalised recommendations before you make decisions.
